TYPES OF COLLECTED PERSONAL INFORMATION

We collect customer information to help us improve your experience on Skillpier. We collect the following types of information.

Account information: name, email address, password, age, gender, postal address, phone number, photos, experience and other information you provide to us.

Transaction information: transaction information includes data on coaching sessions and the services you purchase from us. We do not collect or store debit/credit card information and all transactions are processed by third party payment processors. If you are a tutor or coach with us, we will collect your bank account information.

Course information: information relating to your courses, such as course type as well as the time and location you meet with your coach, will be collected.

Information from other sources: We might receive information about you from other sources and add it to your account information. This information may include data regarding your participation in surveys and your communication with us via third party media services. If you refer individuals to us to receive coaching services, we will also keep records of this information.

Mobile information: When you download or use applications created by Skillpier, we may receive information about your location and your mobile device. We may use this information to provide you with location-based services, such as search results and other personalized content. Most mobile devices allow you to turn off location services.

About Cookies: Cookies are unique identifiers that allow us to recognize your device. Skillpier automatically collects this information. If you wish to disable your cookies, please see the details described under “About Your Choices.”

Account

When you launch our mobile application for the first time, we will ask you if you allow us to track your location information while the application is in use. You can change your decision at any time by changing the location preferences on your mobile device.

Cookies and Web Storage

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies and web storage by default. The Help feature on most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, or how to disable cookies altogether. Additionally, you can disable or delete similar data used by browser add-ons, such as Flash cookies, by changing the add-on settings or visiting the manufacturer’s website. We recommend that you turn on Cookies and Web Storage as they allow you to have the best experience with the Skillpier services, such as Shopping Cart.

If you do not want to receive any promotion or advertisement emails from Skillpier, you can always unsubscribe by using the link in our email. However, we will still contact you with any information or concerns regarding your account.

Mobile application notification

The automatic setting is for you to receive mobile notifications. However, you may at any time change the mobile notification setting in order to stop receiving our application notifications.
