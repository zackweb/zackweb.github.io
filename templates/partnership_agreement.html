Partnership agreement

Party A：Skillopedia Corp

Party B：Applicant

     This partner agreement is between Skillopedia Corp (or Skillopedia) and Party B. Party B will cooperate with Skillopedia as a business partner, and join in the operation of the Skillpier platform.

Platform Charge

1.     Annual membership fee

Annual membership fee is waived

2.     Session commission

  Skillpier platform will take 10% of session price as commission and 3% Visa fee. Every natural month as a cycle, if B’s session income exceed $400, the part exceed $400 will not be charged for commission, but we will still charge for 3%’s Visa fee.

3.     Coupons

   If coupons are published by Party B, then B will pay for these coupons, and vice versa.

Payment Method

   Every two weeks, Skillopedia will pay Party B the total amount for the completed sessions. Party B may choose between Paypal and business checks for the payment method. 

Partnership Obligation

As a partner with Party B, Skillopedia is required to:

1.      Promote the Skillpier market and allow Party B to receive additional clients via Skillpier.

2.      Help Party B create his or her own platform account and sessions on Skillpier, and assist Party B with highlighting and displaying professional information and specialties.

3.      Consult and assist Party B with session management, thereby improving Party B’s competitive capacity.

4.      Assist Party B with completing session. If Party B has conflicts with a client in the session, Skillopedia should help Party B to communicate with the client and solve the problems.

5.      Remind Party B about responding to clients if Party B does not response to booking requests promptly, and also remind both Party B and the client about the time and location of sessions prior to each session.

6.      Offer operational and technical support if Party B wishes to have special offers on the Skillpier platform.

7.       Calculate session payments and send them to Party B in a timely manner.

 

 

As a partner with Skillopedia, Party B is required to:

1.      Be familiar with the processing flow of Skillpier platform, maintain and update his or her published information on Skillpier, including booking availability and teaching location.

2.      Respond to Booking Requests within 24 hours ("Accept" or "Decline").

3.      Contact clients promptly upon receiving booking confirmation.

4.      Remind clients with time and location before session begin.

5.      Show up on time (or early) for scheduled sessions.

6.      Prepare the appropriate equipment for the session.

7.      Actively communicate with clients who have booked sessions.

8.      Provide clients with at least 24-hours notice in the event of a cancellation.

9.      During the session time, protect the safety of the client. Training should not lead to any physical injury or harm of the client.

 

For the safety and integrity of our community, we do not allow:

1.      Exchange of contact information before a booking takes place.

2.      Accept payment from Skillpier clients outside of the Skillpier platform.

3.      Encourage Skillpier clients to book sessions with you outside of Skillpier.

4.      Unprofessional communication or behavior.

5.      Falsification of personal information.
